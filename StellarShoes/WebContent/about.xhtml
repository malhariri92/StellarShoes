Let Us Introduce Ourselves

Shoes can make or break a man! At Stellar Shoes we understand that. That is why we provide top of the line service in helping you buy your next pair. Founded in 1912, Stellar Shoes is a U.S. based retailer of premium men’s footwear. Our elegant dress and casual shoes are made of premium materials and our customer service is legendary. We’ve attracted generations of loyal customers and garnered awards as the #1 men’s shoe store in the region. For four generations, we have enjoyed helping you find the right pair of shoes for everyday wear or that special occasion. We aim to step forward with you towards what’s ahead.
